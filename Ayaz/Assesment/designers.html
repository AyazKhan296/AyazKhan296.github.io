David Carson

David Carson is a world renowned designer who is the father of grunge, widely known for his poster & magazine designs with over 230 awards. He changed the design industry by breaking the rules of image placement, consistent typography and explored elemets such as reverse reading. One of his most famous designs was for Raygun.

Michael Beirut

Michael is an american designer, design critic and a teacher.His designs are known to be convey information in a manner that was easy to understand and read, he doesnt have any particular style but incoporates text in most of his designs.

Saul Bass

Saul bass is an american designer and a filmaker who is known for is work in film posters,films and logos.His style inlcuded cut out animation, geometric shapes and symbolism and revolutionised title credit in films. His best works iclude anatomy of a murder and his film 'Why Man Creates' which won him an oscar.

Max Meidinger

Max Meidinger is one of the most famous typographers in the world, renowned for creating Helvetica. He got the inspiration from Akzidenz Grostik, and wanted to design a new sans serif font that could compete in the swiss market, a font without any additional meaning, and in 1960 Max created Helvetica. Its popularity rose around the mid 20th centuary for its simiplicity. Many major companies such as Jeep, Toyota,Harley Davidson, etc use this font. Fun fact helvetica was originally called neue hass grostik.

Alex Trouchut

Alex is a spanish artist and typographer born in 1981,Barcelona. He has been renowned for his intricate and detailed typography art.His admiration of his grand-fathers modular typographic and ornament system, he set out to create unique and elegant logos for big companies such as Audi and Coca Cola.Apart from major comapanies he has also designed and created albulm covers for the famous singer Katy Perry such as 'Roar'. He makes unique use of shapes,tubes and ribbons to create intricate and pleasant designs.
